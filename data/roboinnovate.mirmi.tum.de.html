robo.innovate
We support robotic enthusiasts and entrepreneurs.
  • Learn more

The hub to foster robotic start-ups in Bavaria and beyond.

True to our motto: “inspire – connect – grow”, we at robo.innovate bring together founders, start-ups, scientists, industry and investors on their journey to develop intelligent robotics. We know the challenges of evolving complex hardware systems and tailor-made software and thus provide targeted support in the implementation of your ideas.

Besides entrepreneurial training programs or access to unique workshop infrastructure robo.innovate teams benefit from a domain-specific community. This makes us unique in the Bavarian start-up landscape. As a partner from industry and research, you have the opportunity to invest in a rapidly growing field of the future and benefit from an immersive environment.

Explore your opportunities. This is how we help you.

  • Students & Researchers

    You describe yourself as a tinkerer and thinker? You have always been fascinated by robotics? You are a successful scientist, but have never considered having your own business?

    Then our education programme for students and researchers is the ideal place to start. We offer workshops on hardware and software design as well as entrepreneurial trainings tailored to robotics.

    Contact us

  • Start-ups

    You are working on a robotic idea, but the initial step towards founding is missing? You are in the process of founding your company and need help with a specific robotic topic? You lack the space and infrastructure to bring your product to the market?

    Then explore our start-up community and domain-specific training about financing, founding and legal issues or get access to our lab infrastructure.

    Learn more

  • Partner & Community

    At robo.innovate, we are a community of selected experts in robotics or start-up financing. Our partners are active in the fields of automation, autonomous mobility, perception or sensor technology. We love supporting talents and forward-looking ideas.

    Together we believe in robotics as a technology of the future, we actively want to shape and advance within society.
