My background is not in business, can I still join?

YES! Our hackathon is open to people from all backgrounds - no matter what's the field or degree of Your studies. Diversity drives innovation!

Do I need to have a startup idea or team already in place?

NO, you donât need a pre-formed idea or team. Part of the hackathon experience is team formation, where youâll meet like-minded individuals and collaborate on fresh ideas during the event.

What is the topic of the hackathon?

This years topic for START's flagship events is Build lasting change in the tech scene. As the topic is our inspiration, it doesn't set unnecesary boundries, and that's what you can be expecting at Hack START Munich 2024.

what's the cost?

The Hackathon is completely free, including food, goodies and resources.

Will food be provided?

Yes, there will be plenty of free food and drinks during the event!

How do we register as a team?

To register with your team, simply fill out the question box with Names and Surnames of your teammates in the registration.

Do you provide travel reimbursements?

Unfortunately, we cannot provide travel reimbursements.

What is the team size?

All teams should be in the size of 3-4 members.

Do you provide accommodation?

We do not provide accommodation, but there will be sleeping rooms at the venue, where you can sleep in/on your sleeping bags/mats.
