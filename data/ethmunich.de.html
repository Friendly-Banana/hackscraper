PretzelDAO Hackathon Manifesto

We are PretzelDAO — a community of builders baked in Munich, at home in web3.

At ETHMunich Hackathon we are searching for something other than the next hyped NFT collection, another wallet project, or token-trading application.

Instead, it is an event dedicated to envisioning the future of the blockchain and exploring untapped concepts and applications which solve real-life problems that can be useful and exciting for people outside of the blockchain space, the “non-veterans”.

Our goal at the hackathon is to bring together experienced blockchain hackers with talented students to solve real-world problems with the help of blockchain technology.

Join us at ETHMunich in-person to be part of the first Ethereum-focused hackathon in Munich and challenge the status quo!

FAQ

  • Is the event free?

    Yes, the event is free for attending hackers.

  • Can I join the event remotely/online?

    No, the event is 100% in-person.

  • Can I participate in the hackathon with a team?

    Yes, you can participate in the hackathon in teams of 3-5 people. Please apply via our homepage individually. You will be able to register as teams on our project submission platform during the hackathon.

  • What is a hackathon?

    A hackathon is an event where software developers, designers, and other professionals collaborate intensively on a software project, often over a short period of time, such as a weekend. The goal is to create a functioning product or prototype by the end of the event.

  • Who is this event for?

    This event is for builders, developers, students (CS, business, HCI), and designers who want to experiment and build with blockchain technology and learn from each other.

  • Will there be talks during the event?

    In contrast to a conference, there will be no talks but workshops from our sponsors and bounty providers, where attendees can learn how to build on the technology they provide. In addition to that, we will have an opening and closing ceremony.

  • Do you provide travel scholarships?

    We don’t provide any scholarships this time.

  • Will there be food and drinks provided during the event?

    Yes, food (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and non-alcoholic food, as well as coffee/tea will be provided for free at the event, thanks to our sponsors!

  • Is the hackathon open to building apps on different blockchains?

    The ETHMunich hackathon is focused on the EVM and EVM-compatible apps. Our judges and mentors can help you with Solidity and Fe Lang.

  • How can I check my registration status?

    Upon acceptance, you will receive an email notification with additional information about the event's schedule.
