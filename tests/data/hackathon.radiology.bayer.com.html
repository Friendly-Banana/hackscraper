Chance to become part of the Beta testing community

Since it will be impossible to develop an entire PoC in 20 hours (happy if you prove us wrong ;)) we designed specific quests from real healthcare AI development cases that you need to solve with the platform. This platform is in its beta version. So we know it will not be perfect and we know there will be bugs. We want you to uncover those & help us design features that make you even better in the future. We also want to know what you love about this new technology. There will be prizes in different categories:

  • most quests solved
  • most bugs found (e.g. Security bug, UI/UX bug, Functionality bug)
  • best feature envisioned (quality of the idea, potential impact, ease of implementation)
  • Best 120 second elevator pitch (if you would need to sell the platform or convince your boss to pay for it, what would you say?)

There will be judges. And they will be from Bayer & Google Cloud.

Legal terms

  • Registration does not guarantee a spot at the hackathon; an independent jury will evaluate all registrations.
  • The hackathon is open to data scientists, specifically non-healthcare professionals in Germany. Participants must confirm that they are not healthcare professionals (HCP) or affiliated with a healthcare organization (HCO).
  • Participants are required to adhere to a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) prohibiting the disclosure of any features, functionality, or proprietary information shared during the hackathon. Specific event-related content must not be disclosed on social platforms.
  • Participants must comply with data privacy regulations and ensure secure handling of any data provided during the event.
  • Any data provided for use during the hackathon is to be used solely for the event's purposes and must be deleted at the hackathon's conclusion.
  • The organizers will provide cloud computing resources for the duration of the hackathon, and participants must use the resources responsibly and adhere to specified usage guidelines.
  • Participants are not allowed to bring existing projects or pre-developed solutions to the hackathon; all work undertaken during the event must be original and created specifically for the AI Innovation Platform Hackathon.
  • Any envisioned features are intellectual property (IP) of Bayer.
