FAQs

What is the Circular Hackfest?

It’s a major hackathon where diverse individuals collaborate intensively to develop innovative solutions promoting sustainability, resource efficiency, and the principles of a circular economy. Up to 150 participants will come together for this unique event.

What is new this year?

There are no predefined challenges or teams. During the kick-off event on Monday, participants will form teams and define their own challenges within the circular domain. There are only two requirements:

  1. Teams must ideate in the circular domain.
  2. Teams must have no more than 10 members.
Why is it held at the MakerSpace?

The MakerSpace at Munich Urban Colab provides access to state-of-the-art workshops (Woodworking, Laser Cutting, 3D Printing, Textiles, Electronics, and Metal) for creating professional prototypes. Corresponding courses covering handling and safety are offered for each area and machine.

Who can apply?

We welcome all motivated tinkerers, hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, and makers over 18 years old â€“ students and non-students alike.

How do I register?

Please register using the button above. You’ll be asked about your MakerSpace membership status. Members require no further action. Non-members will receive an invitation via a special link.

Can I apply as a team?

Absolutely! On Monday during the kick-off event, you and your team can connect with other participants to finalize your team.

I can't code/I'm not technical â€“ can I still apply?

Yes! Interdisciplinary teams require diverse skill sets.

Do I have to be there all days?

Yes, attendance during the hackfest week is mandatory. Teams should coordinate internally.

Is there an age limit?

Participants must be over 18 years old.

Is there a fee?

No, the Hackathon is free of charge.

What support is provided?

MakerSpace staff will be on hand to assist you throughout the event.

Can I switch teams or challenges?

While switching is possible, we encourage you to find a team and project that aligns with your interests early on to maximize your contribution and the team’s progress.

Will there be food?

Yes, fresh vegetarian bowls will be provided daily.

How can I prepare?

If you’re not yet a MakerSpace member, please register for the event. After registering, you’ll receive a special link to sign up for a MakerSpace membership. Existing members can prepare by refreshing their skills and taking advantage of available courses before the hackfest.

What happens with our ideas?

Ideally, your solution will present a viable business case. We are keen to support promising ideas through other UnternehmerTUM programs to potentially spin off into startups.

What do i need to have to use the machines during the hackathon

You’ll need a MakerSpace membership and the corresponding machine course certification to use the equipment. We recommend completing the necessary course(s) before the hackfest. While you may not know exactly which machines you’ll need yet, it’s a good idea to consider the possibilities. 3D printing, woodworking, and laser cutting are definitely popular options to consider.

Do i need a MakerSpace membership

Yes! Members don’t need to take any further action. Non-members will receive a special membership signup-link after completing the registration form. In any case, please sign up for the event using the button above.

Do I get access to the Circular Republic Festival 2025 with my participation in the Hackfest?

No, participation in the Hackfest does not include access to the CIRCULAR REPUBLIC FESTIVAL 2025.
